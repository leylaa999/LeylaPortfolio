IllumniGlobe

Reimagining festive ornaments with engaging, interactive experiences.

Overview

The IllumniGlobe transforms the traditional snow globe into an interactive festive experience. Human interactions such as touch, tilt, and motion activate hidden displays and festive states, creating a captivating journey through different scenes.

Key Features

  • Capacitive sensing, accelerometer, and gyroscope for interaction detection.
  • Vibrant NeoPixel displays synchronized with audio and motor systems.
  • Compact gear mechanisms for moving features like Santa figurines.
  • Interactive festive scenes triggered by user actions.

Gallery

Development Process

The IllumniGlobe uses capacitive sensing modules, an accelerometer, and a gyroscope to measure interactions. These inputs trigger vibrant NeoPixel displays, synchronized audio, and intricate motorized systems. Each element was designed to maximize engagement while maintaining a compact structure and reliability.
