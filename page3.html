Green Cosmetics Pump

A sustainable, recyclable design promoting a circular lifecycle.

Overview

The Green Cosmetics Pump provides an eco-friendly alternative to traditional pumps by utilizing recyclable materials and a vacuum-driven mechanism that minimizes waste and extends product freshness.

Key Features

  • Airless spray mechanism for preserving product freshness.
  • Recyclable HDPE materials ensuring sustainability.
  • Ergonomic design tailored to average hand sizes.
  • Clear end-of-life instructions printed on packaging.

Gallery

Development Process

The pump's design incorporates HDPE and MDPE materials, optimizing recyclability and sustainability. Vacuum-driven systems ensure minimal waste, while ergonomic considerations enhance user comfort and interaction.
